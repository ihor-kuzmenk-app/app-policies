Privacy Policy

Puzzle Animals (the “App”) respects your privacy. We do not collect, store or share any personal data. Below we explain what limited information is used by the App and why.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No name, e‑mail, phone number or any personal identifiers.
  • No location, contacts, photos, media or files.
  • No usage analytics or advertising IDs.

2. Information That Is Temporarily Processed

PurposeTechnical Basis
In‑app purchases (unlocking all puzzles)com.android.vending.BILLING is used to show Google Play Billing dialog. Google processes the payment; the App receives a non‑personal purchase token only.
License check / anti‑piracycom.android.vending.CHECK_LICENSE requests Google Play to confirm that the App was legitimately installed.
Network connectivityandroid.permission.INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE are required solely to reach Google Play services for Billing and License check. No other servers are contacted.

3. Local Data

The App stores your game progress (completed puzzles, settings) locally on your device only. Deleting the App or its data will erase this information.

4. Children

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal data from children.

5. Security

No personal data means no remote storage; therefore common data‑breach risks are eliminated. Purchase tokens are validated locally and, if acknowledged, stored in SharedPreferences.

6. Changes to This Policy

If the App ever starts collecting data in the future, this Policy will be updated and users will be informed through Google Play.

7. Contact

Questions? E‑mail us at ihor.kuzmenk.app@gmail.com.

МетаТехнічна основа
